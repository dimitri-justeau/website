Researcher in **Artificial Intelligence** and **Spatial Planning** for sustainable ecosystem management. My research focuses on developing new approaches based on artificial intelligence to provide decision support in ecosystems management. In this era of ecological crisis, we need more than ever to move towards more sustainable societies. Because the underlying challenges are numerous and complex, we need advanced decision-support approaches to explore and identify sustainable trajectories for tomorrow's societies. My research activities are committed to this momentum and mainly rely on computer science, artificial intelligence, spatial planning and landscape ecology.

You can contact me by mail at: *dimitri.justeau\<at\>ird.fr*
